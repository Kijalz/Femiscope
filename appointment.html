Mentoring Adolescents

Volunteer as a Mentor

Research consistently shows that mentorship plays a crucial role in the personal and academic development of young individuals.

Fempowered Collective

Adolescent girls with mentors are 46% less likely to start using drugs.Mentored students have a 55% higher chance of enrolling in college.Girls with mentors are more likely to have a positive self-image and higher aspirations.

Message from the Founder

My first mentee was my niece. After having extensive conversations about her aspirations, I was able to talk her through how to develop and design a working vision board that would serve as a road map to achieving her goals. In the past, I have used and continue to use vision boards as a tool to keep me motivated to achieve my goals; therefore, sharing this tool with my niece and seeing how it worked well in streamlining her studies and aspirations affirmed my decision to mentor more adolescents. Through Femiscope, I hope to reach at least 1,000 girls within the Githurai Community between 2023 and 2024.
